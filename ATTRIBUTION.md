by Taylor Madearmy, licensed under AGPL-3.0-or-later.

Taylor gave permission on Reddit to reuse the slicing work in an independent
project:

>
> **Haunting_Sun3673** (Helix author): "Copy that! I appreciate it kindly!"

**Thread:** [r/snapmaker — comment by taylormadearmy](https://www.reddit.com/r/snapmaker/comments/1sow8kr/comment/ow24qlg/)

That permission is appreciated and documented here in good faith; it does not
replace the AGPL license terms that apply to the slicer components and engine.
